This is the recipe for a delicious pizzas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 150g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven, slice, and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
