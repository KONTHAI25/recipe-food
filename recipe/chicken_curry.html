This is the recipe for a delicious Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato puree, coconut milk, and curry powder.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season with salt and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Enjoy your homemade Chicken Curry!
